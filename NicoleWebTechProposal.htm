Nicole Chandrawidjaja's Project Proposal

My Objective

The purpose of my website is to create a review of matcha around Boston, Japan, and anywhere I travel within the next few months. Whether it be desserts, matcha lattes, or any variation of the sort, this website will be curated to provide insight to the best matcha spots in town and also delve deeper into its history before it became “trendy” in the United States. I will do this by having more pictures and some short videos of matcha dishes or drinks, sorted into different areas and matcha intensity. In addition, I will add a short description of my honest review and rate it out of 5. I will also have a separate section including matcha/food photography. The main goal is to review the taste and uniqueness of the matcha drink or dish and recommend it to others who may come across it.


My Website Target Audience

The target audience for my website could be matcha enthusiasts or cafe hoppers who love trying new things. I want the website to portray my authentic opinions while sharing its health benefits as well. Because the images and videos will show a lot of matcha content, I hope it can attract just food and beverage lovers in general and encourage them to try new things.
