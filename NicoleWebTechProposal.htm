Nicole Chandrawidjaja's Project Proposal

My Objective

The purpose of my website is to present a portfolio of my creative work through short films of everyday life. Whether it be short clips of my friends and I in college, or going cafe-hopping to explore the best matcha in town, this website will be curated to the feeling of nostalgia, music, and warmth: something that I want to illustrate through film. I will do this by having at least five short clips included with a short description of the moment. It will be something like a diary and the main goal is just to tell a story.


My Website Target Audience

The target audience for my website could be film enthusiasts or those who seek comfort in storytelling. I want my portfolio to exude the feeling of “taking it slow”; home and comfort that is often not captured in everyday life. Because the films will mainly showcase food and human interactions, I hope my short films can attract a wide audience of food and film enthusiasts alike. In addition, I am hoping that I can use this project to showcase my skills in the future as I hope to get into the media and entertainment industry.
