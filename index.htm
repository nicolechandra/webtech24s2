About Me

My name is Nicole Chandrawidjaja and I grew up in the tropical country of Indonesia. Currently, I am navigating the academic journey as a sophomore at Babson College , with my sights set on a future where I merge my passions with my profession. My career aspirations lie at the crossroads of Marketing and Operations, specifically in the dynamic realms of media, entertainment, and game design. Growing up, video games and movies weren't just pastimes; they were a portal to countless worlds and stories, shared in the company of my brothers. This early love for digital storytelling sparked a deep-seated curiosity in me about the magic that unfolds behind the scenes in film and game production. Driven by this fascination, I am eager to immerse myself in the creative process, where ideas spring to life and narratives are woven, shaping the future of entertainment and gaming.

My Hobbies
